The BIOS Graduate Student Association seeks to unite and sustain a diverse community of graduate students across both East and West campuses and all BIOS programs (E&E, MCDB, N, ABC). Our mission is to empower students by fostering collaboration, mutual support, and academic and professional excellence. Through representation among departmental leadership, funding opportunities, and inclusive social networks, we aim to help students thrive and shape impactful initiatives. Together, we strive to build a community where every voice is respected and valued and a strong sense of belonging is cultivated for all.
